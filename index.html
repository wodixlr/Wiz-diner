Pizza

Chocolate Mousse Dessert

Our Chocolate Mousse is a decadent dessert made with dark or milk chocolate, cream and sugar whipped to a light and airy texture. Our Chocolate Mousse Dessert is a must try

Isi ewu

Isi ewu, a classic Nigerian delicacy, slow-cooked to perfection with tender goat head, aromatic spices, and fresh herbs. A must-try for all food lovers. Don't miss out on our authentic food sensation.

Shawarma

Shawarma like you've never had. Our juicy shawarma is made with tender chicken or beef, wrapped in soft pita bread with fresh veggies and signature sauce. A taste adventure on the go!!

Sphagetti and Meatballs

A classic Italian dish gets a Nigerian twist. Tender meatballs in rich tomato sauce, served with perfectly cooked spaghetti. Comfort food at its best.

Jollof Rice and Kebab

Our Jollof Rice and Kebab combo is a match made in heaven. Spicy Jollof rice paired with sizzling chicken and beef kebabs. A taste of West Africa, served fresh.

Burger

Suya
